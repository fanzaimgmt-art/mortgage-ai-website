Good to know

Your questions, answered

Is it really free?
Yes — you pay nothing to get matched and see your offers. Lenders and insurers pay us a fee when you choose to work with them. An optional Premium savings-tracker is $7.99/month, and it's free for founding members during the first year.
Will this affect my credit score?
No. Getting your matches does not require a credit check. If you choose to move forward with a specific offer, that lender may check your credit at that point — and they will tell you before they do.
So will lenders spam my phone?
No. Unlike lead-gen sites, we don't sell or resell your number to a network of lenders. Offers come to us, we show you the best, and you decide who to contact. A lender only reaches out after you choose to proceed with them.
Are you a licensed broker?
Mortgage AI is a technology and comparison platform. We work with licensed lending and insurance partners who provide the actual offers and financing. We bring the options to you in your language; we don't pull your credit and we don't resell your data.
What does Premium add?
Premium keeps the AI working after you're funded: continuous savings tracking, insurance renewal alerts, and better-offer notifications, all in one dashboard. It's $7.99/month — free for founding members during the first year.
